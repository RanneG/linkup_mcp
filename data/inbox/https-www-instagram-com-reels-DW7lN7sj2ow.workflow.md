5. Go to ezgif, upload the video, set the frame rate to 30 FPS and it turns your video into 240 individual images
6. Download them as a zip, then drop that entire file into your favorite AI coding tool
7. Then paste in this prompt and watch the AI add a buttery smooth scrolling effect to your site
